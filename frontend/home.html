What is MapMyMint?

MapMyMint is a powerful visual budgeting tool that helps you track and manage your finances with ease. Using interactive sunburst charts, you can visualize your spending patterns across different categories like Housing, Lifestyle, and Savings.

Key Features

  • Visual Budget Mapping: See your expenses in an intuitive sunburst chart
  • CSV Import/Export: Easily import your transaction data and export updated budgets
  • Quick Add Transactions: Add expenses on the fly without leaving the dashboard
  • Theme Customization: Choose from multiple themes to match your style
  • Budget Categories: Organize your spending into meaningful categories
